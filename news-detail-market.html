Gold Price Movement, January 25, 2023

download

Gold has the opportunity to strengthen today, after yesterday ending higher for the fourth session in a row, reaching its highest level since April.

"Gold bulls continue to draw confidence from fears of a US recession and expectations around a less aggressive Federal Reserve," said Lukman Otunuga, manager, market analysis at FXTM.

Gold for February delivery rose $6.80, or nearly 0.4%, to settle at $1,935.40 an ounce on the Comex, its highest-most-active settlement since April 21, according to FactSet data.
